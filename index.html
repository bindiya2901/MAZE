Introduction

Web-based animated logic puzzle game. Player navigates through a grid to collect key and unlock a door. Each level has a 3-minute timer. Hitting a wall resets the player to the start, but the timer continues. Invalid moves (outside grid or door without keys) are blocked. Difficulty increases with larger grid sizes. Player progress is saved with login. Global leaderboard ranks players by fastest completion time.

Instructions

  • Sign in to start playing. Your progress will be saved automatically.
  • Use the arrow keys (or on-screen controls) to move the player up, down, left, or right.
  • The goal of each level is to collect key and then reach the door.
  • You have 3 minutes to complete each level. The timer does not reset after mistakes.
  • Do not hit walls — hitting a wall will reset the player to the starting position and all keys will be lost.
  • You cannot enter the door without collecting key; such moves are blocked.
  • You cannot move outside the grid; attempts to do so will keep you in the same position.
  • After collecting key, move to the door to complete the level.
  • Complete levels faster to achieve a better score on the leaderboard.
  • You can sign out anytime and continue later from the last completed level.
