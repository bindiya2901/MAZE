Introduction

Web-based animated logic puzzle game. Player navigates through a grid to collect a key and unlock a door. Each level has a 3-minute timer. Difficulty increases with larger grid sizes. Player progress is saved with login. Global leaderboard ranks players by fastest completion time.

Instructions

  • Login to start playing. Your progress will be saved automatically.
  • Use arrow keys or on-screen controls to move.
  • Collect the key and reach the door.
  • You have 3 minutes to complete each level.
  • Hitting a wall resets the player.
  • You cannot enter the door without the key.
  • You cannot move outside the grid.
  • Complete levels faster to rank higher on the leaderboard.
